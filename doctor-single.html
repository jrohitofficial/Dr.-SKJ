Introducing Dr. Jha

Dr. Saroj Kumar Jha is a Consultant Pediatrician currently pursuing a Fellowship in Pediatric Cardiology at Fortis Escorts Heart Institute, Okhla, Delhi (India) with an aim of improving overall as well as cardiac care of the children. Dr. Jha completed his MBBS from Nepal Medical College, Kathmandu, affiliated with Kathmandu University in 2013 AD and his MD in Pediatrics from National Medical College, Birgunj, affiliated with Tribhuvan University in 2020 AD. Dr. Jha’s academic and professional development also includes a Post Graduate Program in Paediatric Nutrition (PGPN) from Boston University, USA in 2023 AD, enhancing his specialization in pediatric nutrition.

Dr. Jha is currently enhancing his academic knowledge and skills in field of Pediatric Cardiology aiming to impact in improved patient care in field of Fetal Cardiology, Pediatric Cardiology and Adult Congenital Heart Diseases.

Dr. Jha has worked at various hospital in Nepal (Birgunj and Kathmandu) and has a clinical experience of more than 4 years. He is also involved in various research projects and have published research articles as well.

Dr. Jha is also an active member of several esteemed medical organizations, including the Nepal Paediatric Society (NEPAS), Nepal Medical Council (NMC), and Nepal Medical Association (NMA). His involvement with these organizations reflects his dedication to advancing pediatric healthcare standards and his commitment to professional excellence.
